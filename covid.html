Covid-19

stay home , stay safe & protect others

Each day we hear about more people testing poistive for COVID-19 in our country.The virus that causes COVID-19 can spread from person to person through droplets that are expelled into the air when an inflected person coughs or sneezes. If you are nearby, these droplets can enter your body through the nose, mouth or lungs.

protect now

take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

make wearing a mask a normal part of being around other people. the appropiate use, storage and cleaning or disposal of masks are essential to make them as effective as possible. clean your hands before and after you take it off, and after you touch it at any time. make sure it covers both your nose, mouth and chin.

learn more

Wash Your Hands

washing hands can keep you healthy and prevent the spread of respiratory and diarrheal infections from one person to the next. to prevent the spread of germs during the COVID-19 pandemic, you should also wash your hands with soap and water for atleast 20 seconds or use a hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol to clean hands.

learn more

Avoid Close Contact

avoid close contact with people who are sick. remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread virus. stay at least 6 feets(about 2 arms lengths) from other people. keeping distance from others is especially important for people who are at higher risk of getting very sick.

learn more

What Are The Main Symptoms?

people with COVID-19 have had wide range of symptoms reported- ranging form mild symptoms to severe illness. symptoms may apper 2-14 days after exposure to the virus. anyone can have mild to severe symptoms may have COVID-19:

  • fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath

things not to do during covid

public transport, metros, gyms and any other crowded place should be avoided. the more the number of people, the more likely of the spread of the virus. if you have symptoms, self-quarantine yourself.

  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Contact Sick People

how to limit the spread of COVID-19

  • stay home

  • avoid public spaces and social gatherings

  • stay at least 6 feets away from others if you must go out

  • wear a face mask

  • wash your hands well and often. use hand sanitizer when you're not near soap and water.

  • try not to touch your eyes or nose.

  • don't attend or host gatherings

  • avoid mass transit

  • consider using delivery services for groceries,medications and other household items

  • cover your mouth with your elbow when you cough or sneeze, or use a tissue

  • clean and disinfect places and things you touch a lot.
